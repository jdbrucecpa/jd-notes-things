Factual meeting record. What was discussed, decided, and committed to—nothing more.

Capture: topics, decisions, context, numbers, dates, names.

Exclude:
- Direct quotes (separate template)
- Sentiment or relationship dynamics (separate template)
- Insights, observations, or tough questions (separate templates)
- Editorial commentary or judgments
- Summaries or takeaways beyond the final 2-sentence recap
- Inline action items or commitments (hold these for the Commitments section at end)

Style: Telegraphic bullets. Fragments over full sentences. No elaboration.
